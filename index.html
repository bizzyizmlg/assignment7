How to custom
build your pC


Parts you will need:


  1. Mother Board

  2. Processor

  3. Graphics Card

  4. Memory

  5. Power Supply

  6. Fan

  7. Tower Case

How to build:

1. First start with motherboard. Make sure you have the compatable motherboard to processor.

2. Connect the processor onto the Motherboard.

3. Apply thermo paste to processor once connected to motherboard.

4. Connect the memory gards to motherboard.

5. Connect the fan to processor and motherboard.

6. Place motherboard into tower and screw in.

7. Place power supply into tower and screw in. Connect power supply to motherboard, graphics card, and possibly the fan. Power supply can be connected either later or before some of these processes.


8. Connect graphics card. Graphics card will be connected to power supply and mother board.
